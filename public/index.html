OILY SKIN

Opt for oil-free, mattifying products that regulate excess sebum production. Gel-based moisturizers, clay masks, and products with niacinamide can help control shine and prevent breakouts.

Know More

DRY SKIN

Choose rich, hydrating products with ingredients like hyaluronic acid, glycerin, and ceramides. Cream-based cleansers and heavy moisturizers help lock in moisture, while gentle exfoliators can smooth dry patches.

Know More

ACNE SKIN

Look for products with salicylic acid, benzoyl peroxide, or tea tree oil to help clear pores and reduce acne-causing bacteria. Lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizers and gentle cleansers are best to avoid clogging pores.

Know More

COMBINATION

Use products that balance oil and hydration, like lightweight moisturizers and gentle cleansers. For targeted care, apply mattifying products to oily areas (like the T-zone) and hydrating products to drier areas.
